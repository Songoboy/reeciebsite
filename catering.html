Catering

Island Wide Service

ReecieB’s exists to fill everyone’s ‘opu with onolicious food. Kama’aina and visitors have many choices when selecting their next meal. With so many choices for a plate lunch, ReecieB’s sets itself apart by serving memorable childhood favorites from backyard luaus to weekends at the beach. ReecieB’s satisfies your taste bud's craving for home cooked comfort foods. Broke the mouth plates delight not only the palette but the overall experience of enjoying a great meal. ​ReecieB’s creates delicious catered meals so you can enjoy the wonderful company of ‘ohana (family) and friends at any occasion, from a small family gathering to large wedding celebration. Affordable menus can be constructed to meet the needs of any budget. Our staff are experienced at developing a cost effective solution for large scale meals to gratify all your guests. Our core menu focuses on local Hawaiian, Filipino and Puerto Rican food recipes fused other local kine grinds like poke bowls and poke nachos. If you are just looking for an ’ono meal, it is easy to find ReecieB’s during the week or weekend at “Pop-Up Stands” like Eat the Street, Kapolei Commons, Taste of Waipahu, local farmer’s markets and other events. Please check our calendar and come visit us. ReecieB’s Onolicious Local Food Truck is coming soon to Kapolei. We will serve daily Filipino, Puerto Rican, and Hawaiian food specials. In addition to our plate lunches, customers can enjoy poke bowls and nachos.
