Our Story

Charisse & Alfred Freitas III

ReecieB’s was established June 2018 by Charisse and Alfred Freitas III. The business name is a combination of Charisse’s nickname, given by her Aunty Bobby, Reecie and the first letter of her middle name. The idea to start a “pop-up” food stand at farmer’s markets was a result of Charisse and Alfred’s passions to cook and fill everyone’s stomach with onolicious food. Charisse’s grandmother co-owned a Filipino/American restaurant in the late 50’s, early 60’s. Her grandmother taught each of her grandchildren how to cook and passed her love for cooking to all of them. Many days were spent in the kitchen learning everything grandma knew. Assisting family and friends with preparing food for large parties has always brought joy to Charisse and Alfred. It is no problem for them to make food for 500 plus people. Being empty nesters has allowed them to follow their passion and open their food business. They are so happy to be able to prepare meals with lots of love.
